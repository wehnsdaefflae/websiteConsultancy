About Dr. Wernsdorfer

A distinguished interdisciplinary AI professional who bridges the gap between foundational research and practical applications.

Dr. Mark Wernsdorfer combines a unique background in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Philosophy of Mind, and Cognitive Science. His career is characterized by deep theoretical investigation into AI foundations and the development of impactful, real-world applications.

From clinical decision support systems that improve patient safety to AI tools fighting disinformation, his work demonstrates how philosophical understanding of AI's capabilities can guide practical innovation.
