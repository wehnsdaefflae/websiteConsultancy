§ 03 — What happens next

What happens after you write.

  1. 01

    I read — carefully.

    Within 48 hours. If your topic is clear, I propose two slots directly. If not, I come back with 2 – 3 follow-up questions.

  2. 02

    We talk — 30 minutes.

    Free, by video or phone. By the end you know whether AI actually addresses your topic — and whether I'm the right partner.

  3. 03

    I send a proposal — or I don't.

    If it fits, you get a concrete proposal with fixed price and clear scope within a week. If not, I say so plainly — and I'm happy to refer you on.
