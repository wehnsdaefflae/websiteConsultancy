§ 05 — Straight answers

Three questions I always hear.

q_01.md
"Can't you just send a proposal?"

No. I need 30 minutes with you before I can tell you whether and how I can help. Anything else would be guessing. And guessing is exactly what breaks AI projects.

q_02.md
"Do you work with enterprises?"

Yes — but only with a clear internal sponsor and a procurement team that isn't building a framework monster. I turn down 90% of enterprise enquiries. The rest, I ship.

q_03.md
"Guarantee it works?"

Guarantee a specific outcome from a probabilistic system? No. But: clear eval criteria, weekly transparency, and a contractual exit after every phase if it doesn't pan out.
