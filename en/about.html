§ 01 · Profile

Interdisciplinary AI expertise.

Dr. Mark Wernsdorfer is an interdisciplinary AI consultant combining profound theoretical research in AI foundations — symbol grounding, consciousness, phenomenology of computation — with practical expertise in machine learning and clinical decision support.

His unique approach bridges philosophy of mind, cognitive science, and real-world AI implementation for SMEs and research institutions. Work moves between foundational inquiry and the construction of deployed systems in environments where correctness matters: clinical laboratories, editorial newsrooms, classroom software.

§ 03 · Approach

Three commitments that define the practice.

01 / 03

Symbol-grounding foundation

Doctoral research on how machines develop conceptual understanding continues to inform which problems are tractable and where current AI systems quietly fail.

02 / 03

Phenomenological method

Applying phenomenology and cognitive science to AI challenges — not as ornamentation, but as a working instrument for more robust and ethically-grounded solutions.

03 / 03

Art & ethics integration

Creative work — AI installations, workshops, public exhibitions — as a way of surfacing and addressing societal implications that pure engineering cannot.
