Professional Summary & AI Consulting Focus

Dr. Mark Wernsdorfer is a distinguished and interdisciplinary AI professional, researcher, and communicator with a robust background at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Philosophy of Mind, and Cognitive Science.

His career is characterized by a unique blend of profound theoretical investigation into AI foundations (such as symbol grounding and consciousness), development of impactful, real-world AI applications (particularly in clinical decision support and for SMEs), and a commitment to translating complex AI concepts for broader understanding and strategic implementation.

As an AI consultant, Dr. Wernsdorfer offers a rare combination of deep technical expertise in machine learning and data science, a philosophical understanding of AI's capabilities and limitations, and practical experience in bringing AI projects from concept to deployment.
